Our experts then assist in selecting an appropriate location while balancing budget constraints. Once the location is determined, we proceed with custom logo design and website development. Following this, we implement web marketing and direct marketing strategies to effectively promote awareness in the target area.

Beyond design and branding, we also handle interior setup and preparation of teaching materials, ensuring all necessary arrangements for school operation are completed smoothly. We provide customized floor plans based on the owner's requests and arrange interior construction and communication infrastructure. In this way, VISION UP's consulting supports the procurement of essential infrastructure for school operation.

Furthermore, we provide specialized advice regarding employment contracts for school managers, instructors, and support staff. Our HR consultants offer expertise on candidate selection and interview strategies, assisting in building the ideal team. If a bus transportation service is required, we assist in creating optimized bus routes between schools, daycare centers, and homes.

By employing detailed local analysis and a hybrid sales approach, we achieve marketing that attracts and engages customers.

VISION UP's work does not end with business development and initial customer acquisition. Through regular management meetings, we review our clients' business performance and provide ongoing advice for improving marketing strategies and operational methods to ensure continued success.
