Our Curriculum
Learning a second language is most effective when it is enjoyable. Our program is designed to meet two key needs: providing a safe environment for children and delivering high-quality English education that parents can trust.

Under our “Learn with Fun” philosophy, we integrate practical learning methods such as CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning) to create exciting educational experiences. Our curriculum, developed in collaboration with Professor Tadayoshi Kaya, a leading applied linguist specializing in early English education, ensures the highest standards in English learning.


Learn with Fun
To make learning enjoyable, our educational methods incorporate the following strategies:

- Colorful textbooks and materials: Designed to make learning feel like play.

- Challenges and competition: Encourages children to challenge themselves, fostering a sense of achievement and personal growth.

- Active learning: Physical activities and movement-based exercises make language learning an engaging and immersive experience.

Through these approaches, children who may feel hesitant or struggle with English gain confidence in speaking. Engaging activities help students acquire English skills naturally, making learning both fun and effective.


CLIL Education
CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning) combines subject learning with practical English skills. Our CLIL approach enhances language learning by integrating English into the following subjects:

- Science & Crafts: Hands-on experiments and creative projects help children learn English in a practical, engaging way.

- Dance & Exercise: Movement-based learning reinforces key vocabulary and verbs dynamically.

- Cognitive Development: Activities that enhance English proficiency while also fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

These activities not only develop practical English skills but also expand knowledge across different subjects. By aligning with students’ interests, our program boosts motivation and nurtures natural communication skills.

Building a Strong Linguistic Foundation
Mastering grammar is essential for language proficiency. Our structured approach ensures that students not only enjoy learning English but also develop a solid understanding of grammar.

- CEFR-Based Learning: Our curriculum follows the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) guidelines for structured language progression.

- Level-Based Curriculum: Grammar and vocabulary are tailored to each student’s proficiency level.

- Balanced Development of the Four Skills: Reading, writing, speaking, and listening are taught in an integrated manner.

- Repetitive Learning: Various reinforcement techniques ensure long-term retention.

- Phonics Integration: Starting with fundamental phonics, students gradually develop reading comprehension skills.
Note: CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) is an international standard for measuring foreign language proficiency, providing a common framework across different languages and countries.


Supplementary Materials and Lesson Plans
Our lesson plans, created by experienced instructors, follow the “Learn with Fun” motto, incorporating CLIL and Focus on Form approaches. By enhancing lesson plans, we ensure high-quality instruction. Additionally, the VISION UP curriculum provides carefully selected supplementary materials, including internationally recognized textbooks and level-appropriate reading resources, to enrich students' overall learning experience.

Introduction to VISION HUB
VISION HUB is a web application designed to facilitate seamless communication between educators and parents using our services. It provides essential tools for daily curriculum management and student learning plans. Developed with user-friendliness in mind, VISION HUB operates on AWS and can be accessed securely on computers, tablets, and smartphones. By delivering streamlined content, it improves operational efficiency.

Daily Curriculum Content
With VISION HUB, educators can efficiently prepare for lessons by easily accessing the curriculum. Upon logging in, instructors can view all scheduled lesson plans for every level. With just one click, they can access all the necessary materials, making it easy to find what they need. Additionally, teachers can quickly review upcoming lesson plans and materials, allowing for structured and well-prepared lessons. In a fast-paced educational setting, VISION HUB minimizes the stress of preparation, enabling educators to maximize productivity.


Student Information and Test Scores
VISION HUB includes a database that stores essential student information, such as age, address, and contact details. This database allows educators to track students’ English proficiency progress and create personalized learning plans to support their language development. It also enables quick communication with parents in case of emergencies.

Furthermore, the system records students’ test scores and daily performance, making it easy to track their progress even when staff members change. By analyzing test history, educators can identify students’ strengths and weaknesses, allowing them to focus on areas that need improvement and further enhance their English skills.
