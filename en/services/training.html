TRAINING.

Learn about the training programs offered by VISION UP

At VISION UP, we offer training programs designed to ensure the success of educational businesses, whether launching a new school or expanding an existing one. Our carefully planned training and support system guarantee that every team member can grow and develop through our programs.

Staff Development

Managers:
Manager candidates undergo training to develop the skills necessary for managing daily operations, acquiring new customers, and overseeing staff. This training helps cultivate leadership abilities from the early stages, enabling them to effectively manage their teams.

Native (Foreign) Instructors:
Training covers phonics instruction, classroom management, child safety, promotional activities, and communication with parents. Our goal is to equip native instructors with the skills to thrive as professional educators.

Bilingual Instructors:
In addition to the native instructor training, bilingual instructors receive further training in handling parent and student communication in Japanese, managing bus services, and addressing a wider range of needs to enhance their capabilities.

Part-Time Staff:
We provide the necessary skills and knowledge to ensure safe supervision of children during transportation, allowing part-time staff to contribute to the smooth operation of the school.

At VISION UP, we are committed to developing professionals who meet the diverse needs of educational institutions, ensuring both the success and growth of our clients’ schools.

Regular Training Programs

At VISION UP, training is not limited to the initial onboarding phase. We offer regular training opportunities to foster continuous improvement. For example, we conduct workshops for instructors both in-person and online to maintain high educational standards and keep their teaching skills up to date. This ongoing approach helps instructors grow into highly skilled professionals.

Additionally, our consultants visit schools to analyze their operations and suggest areas for improvement. While online assessments are available, on-site visits allow us to provide more tailored consulting based on real-world observations.

To further enhance teaching quality, instructor trainers visit schools to evaluate lessons and classroom management skills. Through regular feedback sessions, instructors can continuously refine their teaching methods.

Beyond these core training programs, we also provide specialized workshops on topics such as effective strategies to improve students' English proficiency and methods for enhancing customer retention. These advanced programs help instructors deepen their expertise and create more engaging lessons that inspire students.

Furthermore, our training programs for instructor trainers support the development of in-house trainers within our clients' organizations. Through customized training and workshops, we help trainers improve their skills and become effective leaders in their institutions.

Finally, our online training programs offer a robust support system to address any questions or challenges our clients may face. We hold monthly online meetings to provide timely follow-ups and ongoing support.

VISION UP’s training services are designed to support every aspect of the education business, from staff development to business consulting. Our ultimate goal is to help create the highest-quality learning environments.
