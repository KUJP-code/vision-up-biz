Overview & Mission of VISION UP
VISION UP is dedicated to building successful after-school English immersion programs for children aged 3 to 12. We advocate for the importance of English education in Japan and provide a safe, ideal learning environment that nurtures children's English proficiency. Additionally, we prioritize creating a system that offers working parents peace of mind.

Track Record & Mission
Since opening our first school in Den-en-chofu in 2016, we have continued to expand, launching multiple new schools each year across Tokyo, Kanagawa, Chiba, and Saitama. With over a decade of experience operating Kids UP, we leverage our expertise to support partners in launching new schools or enhancing existing ones. We offer comprehensive support to our clients in the field of education.
